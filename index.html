Period Power

The Period Power podcast helps women facing period problems to get a handle on their menstrual and sexual health issues, stop being held back by their hormones, and understand how they can use the phases of the menstrual cycle to get what they want out of life.

Cultivating H.E.R. Space

For black woman or woman of color looking for an extra dose of inspiration on the go: join Dr. Dominique Broussard (college professor and psychologist) and Terri Lomax (techie and motivational speaker) as they initiate authentic conversations on everything from fibroids to fake friends.

Permission to speak

Trying to be heard in the moments that matter. Wisdom to use our voice better. Permission to Speak is a fun and fiery romp through how we talk and what our voice says about us with Samara Bay, speech coach to the stars and public speaking adviser back when there was a public to speak in.
